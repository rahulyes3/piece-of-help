Our Mission

Support with dignity, delivered by people who care.

Piece of Help exists to provide direct support to people experiencing need in our community. We focus on kindness first, helping people with food and essential items while preserving dignity, respect, and trust.

Our approach is simple: volunteers identify needs in the community, respond with practical help, and rely on personal contributions and donations to keep that support going.
